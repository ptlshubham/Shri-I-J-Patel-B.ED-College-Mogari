Message From From Principal's desk

Dr. Nusrat Kadri
- Principal,

Charotar Education society (CES) is a pioneer educational trust, which has served the cause of education in India for ten decades. With its farsighted vision, CES planned to start self-financed colleges in the faculty of education. Thus, Shri I.J.Patel B.Ed. College got the proud distinction of being the first self-financed college of Gujarat State. It is my pleasure and privilege to get associated to this great institution as its founder in charge Principal. We launched our journey of the noble cause: nurturing effective teachers through quality education. I can say with satisfaction that in less than a decade we have been able to curve a niche for our institution and established its one of the most respected and reputed institution of Gujarat.

During this process we have always adhered to our principles, objective, vision and mission. We have always conducted value based academic and social programmes to concur with the vision and mission of founder fathers and patrons of great Charotar Education Society, who has always given importance to egalitarian society, through consistent innovation in curriculum development, frequent up gradation of its infrastructural facility and human resources and wide range of diverse co-curricular activities, the college has constantly strived to scale new height of excellence. The highest achievement of the college reached its peak with the college achieving the highest grade in NAAC accreditation CGPA 2.72 ‘B’-grade in 2010 among all the B.Ed. Colleges of Gujarat state.
